Project: KazUTB Digital Library
Date: 2026-05-13
Status: Part 1 complete

## Scope of research synthesis layer (Phase 4)

research synthesis layer (Phase 4) is the final research synthesis layer for research synthesis layer.
It consolidates factual evidence already produced in:

- qa/phase1
- qa/phase2
- qa/midterm
- qa/phase3
- qa/paper-assets

## What Part 1 Covers

Part 1 establishes paper-writing scaffolding only:

1. Research paper structure and section objectives.
2. Section-to-evidence maps across all prior phases.
3. Claims-to-evidence control matrix.
4. Figures/tables mapping and ordering plan.
5. Reference inventory and citation-needed tracker.
6. Writing plan and review checklists.

## What Part 1 Does Not Cover

1. Full final prose drafting for every paper section.
2. New experimental execution or new performance/mutation/chaos runs.
3. New metrics generation beyond existing repository evidence.

## Directory Map

- paper/: paper scaffold and evidence maps
- review/: self-review and academic-quality checklists
- references/: source inventory and citation-needed tracker
- reports/: part-level summary
- TRACEABILITY.md: requirement mapping
- CHANGELOG.md: part-level additions

## Connection to Previous Phases

1. baseline QA layer (Phase 1) provides risk baseline and early methodology constraints.
2. automation and CI governance layer (Phase 2) provides automation/gate governance evidence.
3. Intermediate Empirical Review provides empirical reassessment and comparative structure.
4. experimental evaluation layer (Phase 3) provides performance, mutation, and chaos experiment synthesis.
5. Paper-assets provides publication-ready visual evidence.

## Planned research synthesis layer (Phase 4) Part 2

Part 2 will convert this scaffold into full paper prose:

1. Draft section text with citation placeholders resolved.
2. Integrate final academic references.
3. Finalize discussion and conclusion narrative.
4. Perform full academic review pass and final synthesis review packaging.

# Test Case: TC005

**Title:** Verify Account Lockout After Multiple Failed Login Attempts

**Objective:** Ensure that user accounts are locked after multiple failed login attempts.

**Preconditions:**

- User account exists in the database.
- Account lockout policy is enabled.

**Test Data:**

- Username: valid_user
- Password: invalid_password

**Steps:**

1. Enter valid username and invalid password.
2. Repeat step 1 five times.
3. Attempt to log in with valid credentials.

**Expected Result:**

- Account is locked after five failed attempts.
- User cannot log in even with valid credentials.
- Account lockout message is displayed.

**Priority:** High

**Linked Risk IDs:** R1

# Test Case: TC003

**Title:** Verify admin news edit preserves published visibility

**Objective:** Ensure editing an already published news item keeps it visible on public news pages.

**Preconditions:**

- Admin is logged in.
- At least one published news item exists.

**Test Data:**

- Existing published news item slug and title.

**Steps:**

1. Open admin news management.
2. Edit title/body of a published item.
3. Save changes.
4. Open public /news and detail page.

**Expected Result:**

- Update is saved successfully.
- News remains visible in public listing and detail view.

**Priority:** High

**Linked Risk IDs:** R5

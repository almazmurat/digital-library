# Test Case: TC001

**Title:** Verify News Publishing

**Objective:** Ensure that admins can publish news successfully.

**Preconditions:**

- Admin is logged in.
- News content is ready.

**Test Data:**

- Title: "New Library Features"
- Content: "Details about new features."

**Steps:**

1. Navigate to the admin news page.
2. Enter news title and content.
3. Click the publish button.

**Expected Result:**

- News is published.
- News appears on the public news page.

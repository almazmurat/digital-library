## Why Omitted Figures Were Excluded

- They are table-first artifacts and become less readable when converted to charts.
- They require long textual labels that collide in figure space.
- They reduce paper readability compared with concise charts and diagrams.
- The new defect-detection and quality-gates charts are deliberately summary-only and do not replace the detailed tables.

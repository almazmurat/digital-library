Library Usage Rules
(Правила пользования библиотекой)

These rules govern the use of the KazUTB Smart Library facilities, collections, and digital resources. They are designed to ensure equitable access and a conducive environment for academic research and study.

1. General Provisions

The KazUTB Smart Library serves the academic community, providing access to curated knowledge through both physical and digital mediums. Access is granted to all currently enrolled students, faculty, and authorized researchers.

  • A valid institutional ID card serves as the primary library card.
  • Library privileges are non-transferable.
  • Users must present their ID upon request by library staff.

2. Borrowing & Returns

book

Undergraduate Students

Maximum of 5 items simultaneously.
Loan period: 14 days.
Renewals: 1 (if not requested by others).

school

Faculty & Postgraduates

Maximum of 15 items simultaneously.
Loan period: 30 days.
Renewals: 2 (if not requested by others).

3. Digital Access

Access to licensed databases and e-journals is strictly for academic, non-commercial use. Systemic downloading of complete journal issues or database content is prohibited.

vpn_key

Remote access requires institutional Single Sign-On (SSO) credentials.
